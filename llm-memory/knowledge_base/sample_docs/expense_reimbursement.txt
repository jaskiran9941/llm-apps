TechCorp Expense Reimbursement Policy

1. Overview
TechCorp reimburses employees for reasonable and necessary business expenses incurred while performing job duties. All expenses must be documented and approved.

2. Eligible Expenses

Travel Expenses:
- Airfare (economy class)
- Hotel accommodations (up to $200/night)
- Ground transportation (taxi, rideshare, rental car, public transit)
- Parking fees

Meals:
- Breakfast: up to $15
- Lunch: up to $25
- Dinner: up to $50
- Business meals with clients: up to $100 per person (requires business justification)

Equipment and Supplies:
- Office supplies for home office: up to $50/month
- Software/subscriptions required for work: full cost with manager approval
- Professional books and training materials: up to $200/year

Professional Development:
- Conference registration fees: full cost with advance approval
- Professional membership dues: up to $500/year
- Online courses and certifications: up to $2,000/year

3. Non-Reimbursable Expenses
The following are NOT eligible for reimbursement:
- First-class or business-class airfare (without executive approval)
- Alcoholic beverages (except at approved client dinners)
- Personal entertainment (movies, spa, gym)
- Traffic violations and parking tickets
- Commuting costs to regular office location
- Personal phone bills
- Personal clothing (unless required company uniform)

4. Submission Process
Expenses must be submitted within 30 days of being incurred.

Steps:
1. Log into Expense Management System
2. Create new expense report
3. Upload itemized receipts (required for expenses over $25)
4. Categorize each expense
5. Add business justification
6. Submit for manager approval

Missing Receipts:
- If receipt is lost, provide written explanation
- Expenses over $75 without receipts may be denied

5. Approval and Payment
- Manager approval required for all expenses
- CFO approval required for expenses over $1,000
- Reimbursement processed within 2 weeks of approval
- Payment via direct deposit to registered bank account

6. Travel Guidelines
Booking:
- Use TechCorp preferred vendors when possible (discounts available)
- Book flights at least 2 weeks in advance
- Choose reasonably priced hotels near business location

Per Diem Option:
- For multi-day travel, can choose per diem instead of submitting individual receipts
- Rate: $75/day for domestic travel, $100/day for international
- Covers meals and incidental expenses

7. Corporate Credit Card
Employees who travel frequently may request a corporate credit card.

Benefits:
- No need to use personal funds
- Simplified expense reporting
- Automatic itemization

Responsibilities:
- Use only for business expenses
- Submit reconciliation monthly
- Report lost/stolen card immediately

8. Client Entertainment
When entertaining clients:
- Must have clear business purpose
- Document attendees and discussion topics
- Limit: $100 per person
- Alcohol limited to 2 drinks per person

9. Mileage Reimbursement
Personal vehicle use for business:
- Current IRS rate (65.5 cents per mile for 2023)
- Document: date, purpose, start/end locations, miles
- Not applicable for commute to regular office

10. International Travel
Additional considerations:
- Currency conversion at actual exchange rate
- Foreign transaction fees reimbursed
- Vaccinations required for travel: reimbursed
- Passport/visa fees for business travel: reimbursed
