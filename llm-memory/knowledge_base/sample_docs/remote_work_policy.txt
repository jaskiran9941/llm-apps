TechCorp Remote Work Policy

1. Hybrid Work Model
TechCorp operates on a hybrid work model. Employees are expected to work from the office at least 2 days per week (Tuesday and Thursday are recommended core days). The remaining 3 days can be worked remotely.

2. Eligibility
Remote work is available to:
- Employees who have completed 6 months with the company
- Roles that can be performed remotely (as determined by management)
- Employees with satisfactory performance ratings

3. Home Office Requirements
Remote workers must have:
- Reliable high-speed internet (minimum 25 Mbps download, 10 Mbps upload)
- A dedicated workspace free from distractions
- Necessary equipment (company-provided laptop, monitor, keyboard, mouse)
- Video conferencing capability

4. Work Hours and Availability
Remote employees must:
- Be available during core business hours (9 AM - 3 PM local time)
- Attend all required meetings via video
- Respond to messages within 2 hours during business hours
- Set accurate calendar status (available, in meeting, away)

5. Equipment and Expenses
TechCorp provides:
- Laptop computer
- Monitor, keyboard, and mouse (upon request)
- Software licenses

Employees are responsible for:
- Internet costs
- Home office furniture
- Utilities

6. Security Requirements
Remote workers must:
- Use VPN for all company system access
- Keep work devices secure and password-protected
- Never share login credentials
- Report any security incidents immediately

7. Full Remote Work
Fully remote positions are available for select roles. These positions are posted as "Remote" in job descriptions. Existing employees may request to transition to fully remote with manager and HR approval.

8. International Remote Work
Work from outside the United States requires special approval from HR and Legal due to tax and employment law implications. Such arrangements are rare and evaluated case-by-case.

9. Performance Expectations
Remote work is a privilege, not a right. Employees who do not maintain performance standards or violate remote work policies may have their remote work privileges revoked.
